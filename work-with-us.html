// Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

Who is unwindGTM for?

Mid-market B2B SaaS companies ($5M–$50M ARR) in Fintech, LegalTech, or adjacent sectors — where GTM complexity has outgrown the founding team's ability to manage it manually.

How quickly can you start?

We can typically begin a Health Check within 1–2 weeks of initial scoping. Retainer engagements usually start within 2–4 weeks depending on onboarding requirements.

Do you work with companies outside India?

Yes. We work with mid-market B2B SaaS companies globally — primarily in APAC, US, UK, and the Middle East. Our team has operated across all major markets.

What's the minimum engagement?

The Revenue Audit Call is free with no commitment. The RevOps Health Check is our smallest paid engagement — fixed-fee, 2–3 weeks, fully standalone with no obligation to continue.

Are there lock-in contracts?

No. Our retainers operate on 30-day notice. We'd rather earn your continued engagement through outcomes than lock you in contractually.

What tools do you work with?

HubSpot, Salesforce, Marketo, Pardot, Gainsight, ChurnZero, Gong, Chorus, Clay, n8n, Zapier, and most enterprise RevOps and MarTech platforms. We'll work with your existing stack.
